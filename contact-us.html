What We Do

We repair, install, and maintain residential and commercial garage doors and openers across the Ottawa region. Common requests:

  • Emergency garage door repair (broken spring, cable, off-track door)
  • Garage door spring replacement (torsion and extension)
  • New garage door installation (insulated doors for Ottawa winters)
  • Garage door opener repair and installation (LiftMaster, Chamberlain, Genie, etc.)
  • Garage door maintenance and tune-ups
  • Weather seals, rollers, and track repairs

What to Expect When You Contact Us

  1. Call or text: We'll answer or call you back quickly. Tell us what's wrong (e.g. spring broke, door won't open) and we'll give you an honest estimate over the phone.
  2. Same-day option: For emergencies we can often schedule a technician the same day. We carry common parts on our trucks so many repairs are completed in one visit.
  3. Written quote on-site: Before any work, you get a clear written quote. No hidden fees — the price we quote is what you pay.
  4. Warranty: Our work is backed by a 1-year warranty. We're licensed, insured, and BBB accredited.
Pricing & payment: We accept cash, debit, and credit. Transparent upfront pricing — no surprise charges. Seniors receive a 10% discount on repairs. Free estimates for installation and larger jobs.
