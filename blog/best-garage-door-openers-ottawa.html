Best Garage Door Openers for Ottawa: Belt vs Chain & Smart Options

Choosing the right garage door opener for your Ottawa home comes down to noise, budget, and whether you want smart features and battery backup. Here’s a concise guide.

Belt-Drive vs Chain-Drive

Belt-drive openers use a rubber belt instead of a metal chain. They’re much quieter — ideal for attached garages with living space above or beside. They typically cost $100–$150 more than chain and last 15–20+ years with little maintenance.

Chain-drive openers are durable and cost-effective. They’re noisier but reliable and well-suited to detached garages or when budget is a priority. We install and repair both; installed prices typically run about $650–$750 (chain) and $750–$850 (belt).

Smart / WiFi Openers

WiFi-enabled openers let you open and close the door from your phone, get alerts, and often work with Alexa or Google Home. Many include battery backup so the door still works during power outages — useful in Ottawa’s ice storms. Expect roughly $850–$950 installed for a smart belt-drive unit.

Battery Backup

When the power goes out, a battery backup keeps your opener working. We recommend it for Ottawa homeowners; it’s often included with smart openers or can be added.

Brands We Service
