Our Story: Built on Trust & Quality

Founded in 2020, GDS - Garage Doors & Openers Ottawa began with a simple mission: to provide Ottawa homeowners with honest, reliable, and professional garage door services at fair prices. We saw too many companies taking advantage of customers with hidden fees, high-pressure sales tactics, and subpar workmanship. We knew there had to be a better way.

As a locally-owned and operated business, we understand the unique challenges that Ottawa's harsh winters bring to garage doors – from broken springs in -30°C temperatures to ice-damaged tracks, frozen weather seals, and openers struggling in extreme cold. We've spent five years perfecting our craft, learning every nuance of how Ottawa's climate affects garage door systems, and building relationships with the best suppliers to ensure we use only premium, cold-rated parts.

💡 "We don't just fix garage doors – we provide peace of mind, knowing your home is secure, your family is safe, and your investment is protected for years to come."

What started as a small team serving a few Ottawa neighborhoods has grown into one of the region's most trusted garage door service providers, serving Kanata, Barrhaven, Orleans, Nepean, Stittsville, Gloucester, Manotick, and beyond. We've earned this trust through transparent pricing (no hidden fees, ever), quality workmanship (we don't cut corners), and a genuine commitment to customer satisfaction (your happiness matters more than a quick sale).

Whether it's a 2 AM emergency spring replacement, a carefully planned garage door upgrade to boost your home's curb appeal and energy efficiency, or routine maintenance to prevent costly breakdowns, we approach every job with the same level of professionalism, care, and attention to detail. We treat your home like it's our own because, as your Ottawa neighbors, we take pride in serving our community with integrity.
